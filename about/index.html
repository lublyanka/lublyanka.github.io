About me

I’m Lua, a 36-year-old Delivery and Project manager, originally from Saint Petersburg, based in sunny Málaga, Spain, and currently working with the team at TradingView.

I specialize in decomposing complex tasks, devising effective solutions to problems, and ensuring everything runs smoothly. Some might call me a bit of a control freak or a perfectionist, but I see it as a commitment to building robust, fail-safe systems.

My current passion lies in expanding my expertise in DevOps culture, aiming to streamline processes and enhance efficiency. I bridge the gap between high-level project goals and technical requirements, ensuring that development pipelines are as efficient as the business logic behind them.
